部门介绍

About Our Department

欢迎来到韩江中学升学辅导部门。我们致力于通过提供必要的资源和支持，帮助学生实现学术和职业目标，指导他们走向更高的教育道路。

Welcome to the Further Study Department of Han Chiang High School. We are dedicated to guiding students toward achieving their academic and career goals by providing essential resources and support for higher education.

我们的使命 | Our Mission

赋予学生必要的知识和工具，帮助他们探索并追求本地和海外的升学机会。 赋予学生必要的知识和工具，帮助他们探索并追求本地和海外的升学机会。

To empower students with the knowledge and tools needed to explore and pursue further study opportunities locally and abroad.

升学辅导的工作摘点 Job scope of Further Study Department

  • 学生咨询 Student Consultation

提供一对一咨询服务，帮助学生了解不同的高等教育机构、专业课程以及未来的职业路径。
Providing individual counselling to help students understand higher education options and career paths.

  • 职业规划 Career Planning

指导学生进行职业兴趣和能力的自我评估，帮助他们根据自己的兴趣、能力和职业目标选择合适的专业和学校。
Assisting students in assessing their interests and abilities to choose suitable majors and institutions.

  • 信息资源 Information Resources

提供关于各种高等教育机构、专业、奖学金以及职业发展的信息资源。
Offering resources on institutions, majors, scholarships, and career opportunities.

  • 跟踪服务 Follow-up Services

在学生成功入学后，提供后续跟踪服务，包括适应大学生活建议、学业指导和职业发展规划等。
Offering guidance on adapting to university life and planning for future academic and career success.
